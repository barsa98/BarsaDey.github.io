Understanding how life becomes diverse

I am drawn to the quiet forces that shape existence—evolution, memory, environment, and time.

My work in biological data and medical imaging is one path into this question. My journeys through places, food, and living traditions are another. Together, they form a single pursuit: to understand how complexity becomes meaning.
